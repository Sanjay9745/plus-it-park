2500 students

The Web Development Internship Program at Plus IT Business Park is your gateway to mastering the art of creating dynamic and responsive web applications. Through our program, you'll receive comprehensive training in developing web applications with HTML5 and cutting-edge UI development using tools like Bootstrap and Tailwind. Furthermore, we go the extra mile by offering training in MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) stack development. You'll work with various NoSQL and SQL databases, equipping you with the skills and knowledge required to become a versatile Full Stack Developer, perfectly aligned with the demands of the industry. Embark on a journey that will empower you to excel in the field of web development and meet the ever-evolving needs of the digital landscape
